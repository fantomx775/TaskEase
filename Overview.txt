**App Name:** TaskEase

**Description:** TaskEase is a simple and intuitive task tracker and reminder app designed to help users stay organized and on top of their daily tasks and responsibilities.

**Features:**

1. **Task Creation:** Users can easily create tasks by entering a title, description, and due date. 

2. **Task List:** Display tasks in a clean and organized list format, with options to sort tasks by due date or priority.

3. **Priority Levels:** Allow users to assign priority levels (high, medium, low) to tasks to help them focus on the most important ones.

4. **Task Details:** Users can tap on a task to view its details, including the title, description, due date, and priority.

5. **Reminders:** Implement push notifications to remind users of upcoming tasks. Users can choose when they want to receive reminders (e.g., 1 hour before, 30 minutes before, etc.).

6. **Mark as Completed:** Users can mark tasks as completed, and completed tasks will be moved to a separate "Completed" section.

7. **Editing and Deleting Tasks:** Provide the ability to edit and delete tasks in case the user's plans change.

8. **Search and Filter:** Implement a search bar to quickly find specific tasks by title or keywords. Additionally, allow users to filter tasks by their priority or completion status.

9. **Dark Mode:** Offer a dark mode option for better usability in low-light conditions.

10. **User Accounts:** Allow users to create accounts or sign in using their Google account to sync their tasks across devices.

11. **Offline Support:** Ensure that users can access and manage their tasks even when they're offline, with data syncing once they regain an internet connection.

12. **User-Friendly Interface:** Design an intuitive and visually appealing user interface that makes it easy for users to navigate and manage their tasks.

**Optional Features (for enhancement):**

1. **Categories/Tags:** Allow users to categorize tasks using tags or categories for better organization.

2. **Repeatable Tasks:** Enable users to set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or custom intervals.

3. **Notes Attachment:** Allow users to attach notes, files, or images to tasks for additional context.

4. **Collaboration:** Implement sharing and collaboration features, allowing users to share tasks with others and work on tasks together.

5. **Analytics:** Provide users with insights into their task completion trends and productivity over time.
